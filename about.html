About

Hello There!

I am a Data Science enthusiast, Master's Degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Jamia Millia Islamia University, New Delhi.

I am a self-taught programmer and have a keen interest in Natural Languge processing & Computer Vision. I have a great understanding of how Neural Networks works, & i have worked on various Machine Learning and Deep Learning projects.

I have done lots of case studies on healthcare, autonomous driving, Sign language reading, music generation, and natural language processing.

My Mathematics knowledge includes Linear algebra, Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors, Dimensionality Reduction (PCA), Multivariable Calculus, Transformation Matrix, Linear Regression, Vector Calculus, Gradient Descent, Probability Random process and statistics.

I have a great understanding of Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques such as Supervised Learning (Linear Regression, Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, Random Forest, Support Vector Machines, Naive Bayes, K-Nearest Neighbors, Neural Networks), Unsupervised Learning (K-Means Clustering, Hierarchical Clustering, Principal Component Analysis, Apriori, Eclat), Reinforcement Learning (Upper Confidence Bound, Thompson Sampling), Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN), Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN), Long Short Term Memory (LSTM), Autoencoders, Generative Adversarial Networks (GAN), Deep Q-Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Transfer Learning, Hyperparameter Tuning, Model Selection, Regularization, Boosting, Bagging, Ensemble Learning, Stacking, Dimensionality Reduction, Feature Scaling, Feature Selection, Feature Engineering.

I am also a have a good knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap & Tailwaind.
